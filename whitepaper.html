MNODY White Paper

Draft v1.1

1. Overview

MNODY is a community-driven meme coin inspired by the loyalty and playfulness of our beloved pet dog “Mnody.” The mission is to bring fun, creativity, and decentralization to the Polygon network while building a strong global community.

  • Blockchain: Polygon (POS)
  • Token Standard: ERC-20
  • Ticker: MNODY

2. Vision

Meme coins have proven that culture and community can be as powerful as technology. MNODY combines humor and utility to create a token that’s fun, tradable, and useful for tipping, community rewards, and NFT/game integrations in the future.

3. Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 10,000,000,000 MNODY
  • Initial Mint: 10B (All minted at deployment)
  • Current Circulating Supply: ~5B MNODY
  • Burned: 2.5B MNODY (deflationary mechanism)
  • Vesting: 2.5B MNODY locked for 1 year, then linear monthly release over 24 months for ecosystem growth

Distribution (approx):

  • Community Rewards: 50%
  • Liquidity Pool: 20%
  • Development & Marketing: 20% (vested)
  • Reserve / Partnerships: 10%

4. Utility

  • Community Rewards: Airdrops, tipping, and memes
  • Liquidity & Trading: Listed on decentralized exchanges on Polygon
  • Future Use: Integration with NFT collections, gaming, and staking once community votes

5. Governance

MNODY is fully community-led. Holders can propose and vote on:

  • Future burns
  • Partnerships
  • Marketing campaigns
  • Ecosystem grants

Governance will be handled through on-chain proposals as the community matures.

6. Roadmap

Phase Milestone
Q4 2025 Token Launch on Polygon + Initial Liquidity
Q1 2026 Community Airdrops + Website + Whitepaper Release

7. Security & Transparency

  • Open Source Contract: Verified on PolygonScan
  • Locked Liquidity: Proof of lock and burn addresses are public
  • Multi-Sig Treasury: For community fund management (future phase)

8. Disclaimer

MNODY is a community-driven meme coin created for entertainment and experimentation on the Polygon network. It is not an investment product. Cryptocurrencies are volatile; participate only at your own risk.
